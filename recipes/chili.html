Chili Recipe

How to make Chili

If you want authentic delcious Texas-style chili follow this recipe to a Tee!

Perfect for watching Football or battling the cold chili is a must have staple in any cooks recipe book!

Ingredients

  • 2lbs ground beef
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 can tomato juice
  • 1 can kidney beans
  • 1 can pinto beans
  • 1.5 cups chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup chili powder
  • 1.5 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef in skillet
  2. Transfer meat into pot; Stir in tomato sauce, tomato juice, beans, all your veggies, and all your seasoning
  3. Bring to boil; reduce heat to low and simmer for 1.5 hours
